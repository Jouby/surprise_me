🔄 Restore a purchase

If you purchased Surprise Me Premium and it's not showing up, go to Settings → Restore purchases inside the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

🐛 Report a bug

Found something that doesn't work as expected? Please email us with a description of the issue, your device model, and the iOS/Android version. Screenshots are welcome!
